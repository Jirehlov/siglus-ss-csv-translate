## Usage Rules

- keep only stable or decision-critical entries here
- treat the shared termbook as read-only during normal packet work unless explicit ownership is assigned
- store packet-local discoveries in packet-local notes or handoff until they are ready to be promoted
- do not put generic role labels or descriptive speaker tags into pending-confirmation lists
